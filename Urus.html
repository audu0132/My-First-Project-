Lamborghini Urus

    

Price: Prices for the Urus now range from Rs 4.18 crore to Rs 4.22 crore (ex-showroom). Variants: It can be had in two variants: S and Performante. Seating Capacity: The Urus has the capacity to seat five passengers. Engine and Transmission: Both variants of the Urus get the same engine: a 4-litre twin-turbo V8 engine (666PS and 850Nm) mated to an eight-speed automatic. The Performante variant can complete the nought to 100kmph run in 3.3 seconds and has a top speed of 306kmph. The Urus S does the same run in 3.5 seconds and has a top speed of 305kmph. Features: Common features of both variants include dual touchscreen displays in the centre console, a digital driver’s display, ambient lighting, powered front seats with ventilation and massage function and rear seat displays. Safety: On the safety front, it gets up to eight airbags, electronic stability control (ECS) and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS).
